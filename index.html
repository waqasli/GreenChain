Q1-Q2 2024 Roadmap
Establish Website: March 2024
The journey began with the establishment of the GREEN CHAIN website in March 2024. This initial step was crucial for creating an online presence, sharing the project's vision, and providing users with essential information about GREEN's mission and ecosystem.
Beta Version of Android App: March 2024
Shortly after, in March 2024, GREEN will release the beta version of its Android app. This mobile application marked the first tangible interaction point for users to experience GREEN's unique features, including mining, staking, and the marketplace. It represented a significant technological achievement in providing a user-friendly interface for environmentally responsible crypto enthusiasts.
Beta Version of iOS App: March 2024
The launch of the beta version of the GREEN iOS app, expanding the ecosystem's reach to a broader audience of Apple device users.

This move towards cross-platform compatibility demonstrated GREEN's commitment to inclusivity and accessibility for all.
Peer-to-Peer GREEN Marketplace: March 2024
One of the most eagerly awaited milestones was the launch of the peer-to-peer GREEN marketplace in March 2024.

This marketplace opened up new horizons for GREEN users, enabling them to buy and sell GREEN tokens securely, with a minimum price of 0.01 BNB.

The marketplace represented a pivotal moment for GREEN's economic ecosystem.
